LightSoup

Ingredients

Please lets look closely at the following items used in the preparation of this special meal.

  • Fish/Chichen/Meat
  • Grinded Pepper
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Salt
  • Water

    • Preparation Steps

    1. Wash fish/chicken/meat thoroughly with water and place it in a sauce pan.
    2. Slice onion nicely into your sauce pan.
    3. Add the tomato paste.
    4. Add salt to tast.
    5. Add just small quantity of water.
    6. Allow to boil for a While.
    7. Add Final quantity of water and salt to taste just to suit your consumption in proportion to your ingredients.

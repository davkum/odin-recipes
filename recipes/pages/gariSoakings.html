LightSoup

Ingredients

Please lets look closely at the following items used in the preparation of this special meal.

  • Gari
  • Sugar
  • Groundnut
  • Ice Cubes
  • Salt
  • Water

    • Preparation Steps

    1. Pour gari into bowl and add a lot of water .
    2. pour excess water from bowl.
    3. Add the sugar and salt Note: salt must be very small.
    4. Add groundnut to taste
    5. stir mixture and add extra water if required.
LightSoup

Ingredients

Please lets look closely at the following items used in the preparation of this special meal.

  • Gari
  • Sugar
  • Groundnut
  • Ice Cubes
  • Salt
  • Water

    • Preparation Steps

    1. Wash fish/chicken/meat thoroughly with water and place it in a sauce pan.
    2. Slice onion nicely into your sauce pan.
    3. Add the tomato paste.
    4. Add grinded pepper to taste
    5. Add salt to tast.
    6. Add just small quantity of water.
    7. Allow to boil for a While.
    8. Add Final quantity of water and salt to taste just to suit your consumption in proportion to your ingredients.

Ingredients

Please lets look closely at the following items used in the preparation of this special meal.

  • Fish/Chichen/Meat
  • Grinded Pepper
  • Onion
  • Oli
  • Carrot
  • Cabage
  • Tomato Paste
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Rice

    • Preparation Steps

    1. Wash all ingedients thoroughly with the exception of rice and oil thoroughly with water and place it in a sauce pan.
    2. Slice onion,cabage and carrot nicely into your separate bowls.
    3. Add the oil to sauce pan and put on fire.
    4. Add Fish/Chichen/Meat into sauce pan and allow to fry to taste
    5. Add onion, cabage and carrot and allow to fry to tast.
    6. Add tomato paste and pepper Note: pepper must be moderate.
    7. Add salt to taste and allow all to simmer for a While.
    8. Add Final quantity of water, rice and salt to taste just to suit your consumption in proportion to your ingredients.
    9. Allow to cook to the desired taste.
